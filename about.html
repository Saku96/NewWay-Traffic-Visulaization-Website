About NewWay

Geographical area

Nugegoda and its connected areas such as Maharagama, Kirulapana, Dehiwala, and Battaramulla. There is daily traffic congestion in these areas due to various reasons.

Survey

This website visualizes data which gathered survey from more than a hundred and fifty people on Weekday traffic jams in Nugegoda and its connected areas.

Time based Maps

There are six time periods in the day from 8 AM to 8 PM. These time-based maps visualize high and low traffic areas according to analyzed survey data.

Traffic Visualization Graph

This graph visualize vehicle counts which moving towards from Nugegoda to Maharagama, Kirulapana, Dehiwala and Battaramulla.
